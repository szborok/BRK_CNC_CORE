Center

Create a curve in the center of suitable faces.

Curves → Center

Create a curve in the center of one or more suitable faces (pipes, fillet face and NURBS). Tolerance defines whether a curve is an arc. If you select Include variable radii, the centers curve will also be calculated for faces with a variable radius . Use Check consistency to highlight each start and end point of a centers curve with a red point .

If a face keeps to the tolerance in both the U and V direction along the isoparametric curves, two center curves are created for the face.
