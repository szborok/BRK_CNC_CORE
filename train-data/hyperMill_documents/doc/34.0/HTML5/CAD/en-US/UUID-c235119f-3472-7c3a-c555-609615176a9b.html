Decimate mesh

Reduce data by combining triangles.

Modify → Decimate mesh

Solids

Reduce data by combining triangles based on criteria. Nodes are removed from the mesh. The result is a precise subset of the original nodes. No remeshing operations are performed.

Select

Mesh: Select an entity . If Keep attributes has not been selected, the attributes that are active for new entities will be used. If Keep original is selected, the original entities are retained.

Constraints

Distance: If the distances measured between the middle points of the triangles are less than the value, the triangles are combined .

Normal deviation: If the angle between the normals of the triangle is less than the value, the triangles are combined .

Edge length: If the length of an edge of triangles is less than the value, the triangles are combined .

Aspect ratio: Enter the required ration from the area of the triangle divided by the length of the longest side of the triangle .

Stop condition

Only decimate with the Constraints only option on the basis of constraints. Alternatively, end the process before the Number of vertices or the Number of triangles fall below the minimum values. Enter the number in Target. The current number of entities and vertices is displayed in Current.
