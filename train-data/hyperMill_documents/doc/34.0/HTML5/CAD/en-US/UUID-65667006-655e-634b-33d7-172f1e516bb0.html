Average

Generate an isoparametric curve at 0.5 between two curved curves in space.

Curves → Average

Generate a curve or a chain of curves. These resulting curves follow the course of the isoparameter lines U = 0.5 of imaginary ruled faces. The imaginary ruled faces are clamped between the selected curved curve pairs or curve chains lying in space.

Select

Select two chains of curves Curve A and Curve B As single selection or as chain selection .The number of selected curves is displayed.

More options

Joined curves: The two selected curve chains are joined into a single curve for the purpose of performing the calculation. The resulting curve is calculated on an imaginary ruled face . This curve is divided at G0 continuities (in other words, tangential discontinuities). In other cases, the result is calculated for each selected pair of curves on an imaginary ruled face. Gaps in the curve chains are temporarily marked by red dots and must be closed.

Join resulting curves: Multiple resulting curves are combined into one curve .
