Graphic > System > Highlight

Highlight color: Color for selected (= highlighted) entities.

Alternate highlight color 1 and 2: Alternative colors for entity selection processes.

Highlight thickness: Select the line thickness for highlights.

Dynamic highlight: An entity or a boundary is highlighted in color while it is touched by the cursor.

  • Shaded dynamic highlight: Highlight the entire face instead of just the edge and UV lines of an entity.

  • Dynamic highlight with hidden lines: For boundaries, edges and silhouettes, also show the invisible areas while the cursor hovers over the entity. Dynamic highlight with hidden lines is deactivated for toolpaths.

  • Dynamic highlight color: Select the color for dynamic entity selection in the Color selection dialog.

The settings are stored in the viewViewProperties.xml file.
