Curvature plot

Display a curvature plot to visually check the curvature or curvature radius course of curves and boundaries.

Analysis → Curvature plot

Display a curvature plot to visually check the curvature or curvature radius course of the selected curves.

The boundaries of faces can also be selected in addition to curves.

Curvature radius shows the curvature radius over the course of the curve. Invert reverses the display direction of the curvature plot.

Select

Select the curves . The number of selected entities is displayed.

Scaling

If Automatic is selected, the scale of the curvature plot will be controlled by the system. The size can also be specified by entering a Value or using the slide control.

Visibility

Segments: Shows the segments of the curvature plot .

Shading: The curvature plot is displayed shaded .

Side curves: Show the side boundaries of the curvature plot .

Top curve: Show the top boundary of the curvature plot .
