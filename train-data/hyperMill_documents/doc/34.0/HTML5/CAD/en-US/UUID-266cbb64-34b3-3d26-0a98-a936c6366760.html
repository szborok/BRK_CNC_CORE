Split face

Split one or several faces along isoparametric curves.

Modify → Split face

Select

Faces: Select the faces to be split . The number of selected elements is displayed.

Point: Select a point to determine the position for the split.

Modes

U par., V par.: Splits the faces in the U or V direction at the position of the separation curve. Specify the position via U or V parameters or by positioning the separation curve freely on the face in the preview .

U joints, V joints: Splits the face at the NURBS arc joints in the U or V direction .

Parameter

Enter a value for ‘U par.’ or ‘V par’.

Spans

Select: In the preview, you can specify curves at whose position the faces are to be split.

Options

Keep original: Specify whether the selected face is to be retained and a copy is to be created.

Keep domain: The untrimmed initial face is retained.
