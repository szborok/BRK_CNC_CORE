From curves loop

Select entities within a closed chain of curves.

Select → From curves loop

2022.2

Select entities within a closed chain of curves. All inner entities are marked. The chain can consist of 2D or 3D curves or boundaries. The viewing direction serves as a reference for projecting the chain onto the entities.

Curves: Select a chain of curves along a given shape or outside (for example, ellipse) .

Completely inside: The option allows you to decide whether the entities must lie completely within the chain or may also overlap the chain. If the option is selected, only entities that lie completely within are marked .

Populate: Refresh the preview of the selected entities.
