Blending

Insert a blending face between two faces.

Shapes → Blending

Insert a blending face as a NURBS entity between two faces with a tangentially constant transition (G1) to these faces. Use Weight to influence the shape of the blending face. If a twisted face is generated, use Invert to correct the face .

Select

Select the faces near the face boundaries Boundary A and Boundary B . The face boundary for inserting the blending face is displayed.
