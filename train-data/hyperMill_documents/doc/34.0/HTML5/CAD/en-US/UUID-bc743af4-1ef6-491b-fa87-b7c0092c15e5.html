Light settings

The lighting in the graphics area is controlled using an ambient light source and four directional light sources. The way in which an entity is displayed is not determined simply by these light sources; the color of the displayed entity also affects how it is displayed.

The ambient light provides basic background brightness. Light 1 to Light 4 are light sources with directed, parallel rays of light that each illuminate the model from the direction of a direction vector . This direction vector is entered as a unit vector (X, Y, Z) between -1.0 and +1.0. Directional light has no origin point. The direction of the light can remain constant from the perspective of the viewer despite view changes, or it can move with the model.
