Touch operation

The software is operated on a screen with touch screen function with multi-touch finger gestures.

Zoom in by moving two fingers apart on the screen and zoom out by moving them together again . Swipe two fingers back and forth to move the view .

Rotate two fingers to rotate the view around the normal of the screen . Slide one finger back or forth to rotate around an axis. In order to do this, start outside the displayed entities .

Touch the screen twice (similar to the way in which you double-click with the mouse) to adjust the view in such a way that all the visible entities of the document are displayed .

Touch the screen slightly longer with one finger and drag to select entities within a rectangular selection area . Touch the screen again to remove the selections.

Other input devices are disabled during touch operation. As soon as you begin an action with an input device, other devices are disabled until the action has been completed in order to avoid conflicts.
