Center line

Create center lines on circles and arcs.

Drafting → Center line

Create center lines on circles and arcs. The middle point and boundary are determined by the geometric properties of the selected entity. The lines are either aligned parallel to the axes of the current workplane or via the entity geometry.

Select

Select the circles and arcs on which you want to create center lines . The number of selected entities is displayed.

Reference plane

WP: The center lines are aligned on the basis of the current workplane .

Intrinsic: The center lines are aligned on the basis of the curve, independently of the current workplane .

Rotation

Angle: The center lines are created and aligned by entering an angle .

Direction: Align the center lines with an entity to be selected .

Extension

Length: Extend the end points of the center lines by entering a value (equal) .

Edit lengths: If this option is active and you have only selected one entity, you can extend the lines individually by entering a value or dragging the handles.
