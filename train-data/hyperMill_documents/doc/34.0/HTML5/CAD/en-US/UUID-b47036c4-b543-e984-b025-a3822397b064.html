17.4. Dimensions and labels

The dimensions of the entities are associative, such as for direct modeling. The measure will be adjusted when the geometry is changed. The dimension can assume different states. The state is visualized in the model tree, on the Model tab.

Table 97. Possible states with associative dimensioning

State

Icon

Explanation

Fully linked

In the case of associative dimensioning, the modification of entities, such as for direct modeling, will cause the measure of the dimension to be changed accordingly.

Partially linked

The assignment of a dimension to entities is one-sided. The dependent entity is not modified.

Not linked

The dimension is not related to entities, for example, because the entities have been deleted.



In terms of function, this associative dimension that can be used for V-sketches must be distinguished from the usable dimension, in which case a modification to the measure will adjust the geometry automatically.
