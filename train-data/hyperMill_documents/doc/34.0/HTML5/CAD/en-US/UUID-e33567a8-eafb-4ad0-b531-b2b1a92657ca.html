Invert faces UV-parameter

Invert the U and V direction of isoparametric curves in faces.

Modify → Invert faces UV-parameter

Invert the U and V direction of isoparametric curves and so also change the orientation of faces and faces in solids (open and closed solids). This may be useful, for example, for Boolean operations, in case of import problems, or for CAM programming on the basis of face isoparameters.

The direction of the normal vectors of the faces and the U and V direction are shown with arrows.

The selected entity is converted into NURBS geometry if it is not a planar face or NURBS face. The default settings of the Convert to NURBS command are used (Degree: C2, Tolerance: 0.001 mm, Max. degree: 12, Max. patches: 500, Non-rational). A warning and information about NURBS parameters are output on the Info tab. Depending on the continuity of the initial face, it is not always possible to adhere to the required parameters for the NURBS approximation.

Select

Faces: Select entities . The number of selected entities is displayed.

Invert U: Reverses the U direction of a face. Click the button first, then click the entity with the left mouse button .

Invert V: Reverses the V direction of a face. Click the button first, then click the entity with the left mouse button .

Swap U/V: Exchange the U and V directions of the isoperimetric curves. First, click the button, then left-click the entity .
