Scale

Scale, stretch or shrink entities.

Edit → Scale

Scale entities equally in all directions (isotropic) . Stretch or shrink entities in the X, Y and Z direction using different factors (anisotropic) . The axial direction follows the alignment of the current workplane. The origin of the current workplane is initialized as the origin. Use Keep original to create a copy of the original entities. If Keep attributes has been selected, the copies are assigned the same graphical attributes and lie on the same layer as the base entities. If Keep attributes has not been selected, the attributes that are active for new entities will be used.

Select

Entities: Select the entities to be scaled . The number of selected entities is displayed.

Origin: Choose another origin for scaling the entities .

Scale

Isotropic: Scales the entities globally in all directions when you input a scaling factor or drag the handle in the graphics area .

Anisotropic: Stretches the entities in a directionally dependent manner in accordance with the scaling factor selected for the X, Y, and Z direction or when you drag the handles .
