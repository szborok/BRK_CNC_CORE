From boundaries

Create a face from a maximum of four boundaries.

Shapes → From boundaries

Create a face from a maximum of four boundaries. The Tolerance defines the permissible inaccuracy of the boundaries that delineate the edges of the face, so that gaps between face boundaries or curves are ignored, for example. The tolerance is used to check whether two curves are connected. If the ends of the curves do not converge within the permissible tolerance, the face point is placed in the middle between the two ends. The size can be specified by entering a value or using the slide control. The value range moves between the 'Approximation tolerance' and the hundredfold 'Approximation tolerance'.

Select

Curves: Choose between two and four boundaries (curves, face boundaries and edges of shapes) . The number of selected entities is displayed.

Styles

There are various options you can choose to design the shape when you create a face between four boundaries – Smoothed and Curved.
