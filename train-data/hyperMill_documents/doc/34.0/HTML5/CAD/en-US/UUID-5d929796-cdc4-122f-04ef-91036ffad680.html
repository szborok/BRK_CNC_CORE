Continuity curve

Modify a transition between two individual curves.

Modify → Continuity curve

Select

Curve: Select the curve to be adjusted .

Reference: Select the curve that is to be referenced .

Continuity

Position creates a positional transition between the two curves , Tangency creates a tangential transition and Curvature creates a curvature constant transition . The reference curve is not affected by the modification. The end points of a curve and the start point of the next curve subsequently lie on top of each other.

More options

Propagate: Using a handle or Parameters, set the area of the curve that may be varied to adjust the continuity . The Parameters range between 0 and 1.

Keep original: The initial curves are not removed.

Keep attributes: The modified curves adopt the layers and the colors of their respective initial curves.
