Joblist and electrode feature

A Job list and Electrode feature are created if the Electrode milling dialog is closed with OK. In the job list, the functions Stock, Model and Clamp are activated and the previously created elements are selected.

In the electrode feature, the production-related elements of the electrode geometry, such erosion area, extension, etc. and the stock geometry are saved as feature parameters.
