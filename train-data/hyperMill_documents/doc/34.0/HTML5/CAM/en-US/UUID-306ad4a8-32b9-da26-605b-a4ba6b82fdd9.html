Macros

Define the tool approach at the starting point and the tool retraction from the end point of the contour. This behavior can be defined manually or calculated automatically by hyperMILL.

(A) Internal thread: (1) Quarter circle, (2) Half circle, (3) Perpendicular.

(B) External thread: (1) Quarter circle, (2) Half circle, (3) Perpendicular, (4) Tangential.

Feedrate macros

Define the feedrate for the approach.

Note

The calculation of the Automatic macros is valid only for standard 60 degrees thread profile.
