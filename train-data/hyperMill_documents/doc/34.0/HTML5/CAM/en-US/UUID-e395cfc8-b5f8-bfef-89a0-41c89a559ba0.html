Macros

Approach macro/Return macro

Length: Length for tool approach and retract movements.

Additional axial distance: Increases the approach and retract path of the macros in the tool axis direction.

Axial feedrate

Feedrate: You can define the feedrate for the macro movement in the axial direction separately. It is usually preferable to use a higher feedrate here.
