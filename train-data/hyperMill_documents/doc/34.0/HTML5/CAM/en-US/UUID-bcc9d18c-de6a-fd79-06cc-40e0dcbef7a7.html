5 axes

Lead angle/Automatic lead angle

Desired lead angle (min): (1) Angle of inclination of the tool to the surface in the infeed direction (2)

Automatic adjust: This function prevents a collision with the back face of the milling tool by automatically increasing the specified lead angle.

Constant: Calculates a Lead angle for each path that can be maintained unchanged across the entire path.

Variable: Calculates the best possible fit of the tool face to the surface(s) at all points on the path.

Min. clearance angle / Max. clearance angle: Minimum (3)/maximum (4) angle to be maintained between the underside of the tool and the blade surface to be machined.

Machine limitation

Max. angle to Z: Measured at the Z axis of the frame. It can be used to prevent the output of tilt angles that exceed the position range of the machine when path smoothing or collision avoidance is activated.
