Boundary

Stop surfaces

Stop surfaces: Use stop surfaces to define the area of the model on which no machining takes place. You should use stop surfaces whenever the tool is not allowed to touch the stop surfaces.

Offset: The offset of the stop surfaces extends the area excluded from machining by the specified value.
