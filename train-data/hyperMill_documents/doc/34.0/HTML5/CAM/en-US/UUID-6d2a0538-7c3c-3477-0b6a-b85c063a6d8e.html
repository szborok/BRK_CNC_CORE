5.6. Slot-Rib Probing

Probing of the size and position of slots and ribs with the option to adjust a reference tool and set the origin. The following selection modes are possible: Middle point (1), 2 Points (2) or 2 Lines (3).

Examples of use

(1) Rib, circular, (2) Slot, rotational shape, (3) Rib within slot, (4) Middle point of two holes with rib.

Selection mode: 2 Points
