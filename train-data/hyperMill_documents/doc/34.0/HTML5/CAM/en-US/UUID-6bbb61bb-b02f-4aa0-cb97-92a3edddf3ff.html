The 5-axis parameters

Tilt strategy

Lead angle: The lead angle (1) applies in thee infeed direction (2).

Tilt angle: The tilt angle (3) applies laterally to the infeed direction.

Both angles are relative to the surface normal at the contact point.

Tilt parameters

Desired tilt angle: Tilt angle without consideration for collision avoidance.

Max. angle to Z: Measured at the Z axis of the frame. It can be used to prevent the output of tilt angles that exceed the position range of the machine when path smoothing or collision avoidance is activated.
