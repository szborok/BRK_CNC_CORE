5 axes

Tilt angle

Leading tilt: Angle of inclination of the tool to the surface in the infeed direction. Necessary if a lot of material remains at the transition between the platform and blade surfaces after roughing.

Tilt angle control: Tool axis angle to the X axis of the frame in use. This option allows you to maintain an additional slope angle to the platform surfaces.
