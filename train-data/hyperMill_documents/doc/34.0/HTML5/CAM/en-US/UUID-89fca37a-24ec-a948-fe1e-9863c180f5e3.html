Combine multiple stock models

If you want to machine several components together that each have a separate stock model, you can merge the stock models to form a Compound stock. A component can be machined and checked against the compound stock at the same time.

To create a compound stock:

  1. Right-click the stock browser and select New compound stock.

  2. Then select the required stocks in the list of available stock models and confirm your selection.
