Boundary

Limit the machining area and trim toolpaths on a stock model.

Surfaces

Keep against surfaces: Activate if the addition of material should only take place where the tool is in contact with the selected Surfaces / Meshes.

Important

A mix of Surfaces and Meshes is not supported.

Surfaces: Select the Surfaces / Meshes to consider for the trimming of the path.

Offset: Positive values enlarge the additive area.

Stock

Trim to stock: Enable to use the stock to trim the toolpaths. A defined Offset is taken into account.

Offset: Positive values enlarge the area of the stock.
