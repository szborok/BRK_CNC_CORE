Define turning area

In the hyperMILL browser, go to the Jobs tab and open the required job by double-clicking it. On the Setup tab, select the Collision check option and

click the icon to define a new turning area or

click the icon to modify a turning area.

Alternatively, open the dialog via the New turning area shortcut menu in the hyperMILL bowser on the Models tab.

The Turning area dialog

General

Name / Comment: Specify a name and, if necessary, a comment for the turning area.

Turning area

Contour: Select a (closed) contour to define the collision-checked turning area.

Click the icon to open the Create turning area from model dialog.
