Menu and Toolbar

The ReportDesigner is a typical editor program, i.e. the user uses it to open a file, makes changes to that file, and then saves the results as a file.

After opening the file, an element must first be selected from the structure. This can then be copied, moved and removed. Other functions may be possible depending on the currently selected element, such as editing the specific properties and adding possible child elements.

A separate menu is available to insert specific new elements into the structure, while important elements can also be inserted directly from the toolbar. The Tools menu item contains additional complex functions which may become necessary in some situations.
