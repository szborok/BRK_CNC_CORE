Defining names, selecting material and machine

Finalize setup: Define the names for the Joblist, Milling area / Turning area and the Stock. Select Material and Machine from the list. The turning area is calculated with an accuracy of 0.005 mm / 0.0002 inch.

New elements are created in the hyperMILL browser based on the definitions. The most recently used settings are applied.
