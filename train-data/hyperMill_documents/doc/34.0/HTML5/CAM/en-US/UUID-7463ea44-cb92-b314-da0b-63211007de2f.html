Strategy

Tool position

Selection of the tool position in reference to the selected contour depends on the direction of the contour.

Auto climb: The direction of the contour is automatically adjusted in such a way that machining always takes place using climb milling.

On contour (1): The tool moves directly on the contour.

Left (2), Right (3): The tool moves left / right beside the contour.

Infeed

Oneway (1): Machining is always performed in the same direction.

Zigzag (2): Machining performed with alternating orientation.

Machining sequence

Bottom (1): Machining takes place under consideration of top and bottom initially in the vertical direction, then switches to the next lateral step.

Plane (2): Machining takes place under consideration of the total lateral infeed initially in the lateral direction, then switches to the next vertical step on the next plane.

Graphic: tool reference point: tip.

Interior corners

Fillet interior corners: Filleting of interior toolpaths at the corners of the selected contour. Enter a fillet radius.

Axial infeed

Select the axial machining direction or sequence. Top to bottom (1), Bottom to top (2), Middle, bottom, top or Middle, top, bottom (3).

With the Automatic option, automatically adjust the axial infeed to various machining situations:

Slot closed at top and bottom > machining is performed as with the Middle, bottom, top option.

Slot closed at bottom, open at the top > machining is performed as with the Top to bottom option.

Slot closed at top, open at the bottom > machining is performed as with the Bottom to top option.
