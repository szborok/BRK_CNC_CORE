Macros

Approach macro / Retract macro

The following tool movements are offered for approach and retract behavior: Perpendicular (1), Circular (2), Tangential (3), Ramp, approach macro only (4). The Ramp approach macro is only available when the ramp infeed is not activated.

Approach macro parameter: Ramp height (1), Ramp angle (2), Ramp length (3). The Ramp increment (4) is calculated automatically from these parameters.

The Macro position parameter is not available if Auto climb is used as the tool position.

Feedrate macros

Define the feedrate for approach and retract macros with Feedrate lead in and Retract . Default = Feedrate XY.

Macros normal to surfaces: The approach and retract macros are executed in the direction of the surface normals.
