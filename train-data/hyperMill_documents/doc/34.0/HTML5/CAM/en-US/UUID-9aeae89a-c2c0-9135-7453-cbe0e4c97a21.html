Coupling types

The following coupling types are available in the tool database:

Adaptor ISO 13399: Available for combining Spindle and Holder / Extension. Requirement for combining: Adaptor for spindle and adaptor for top coupling of holder/extension are identical.

Arbor Geometric parameters: Diameter, Length. Combination conditions: - components with an arbor, - names of couplings are identical, - fixed clamping area.

Morse Taper Geometric parameters: Length. Combination conditions: - can be combined with morse taper, - names of couplings are identical.

Side Lock with external thread Geometric parameters: Diameter, Length. Combination conditions: - components with side lock with internal thread, - names of couplings are identical, - external thread completely fits into internal thread, - fixed clamping area.

Side Lock with Internal thread Geometric parameters: Diameter, Max. length. Combination conditions: - components with side lock with external thread, - names of couplings are identical, - external thread completely fits into internal thread, - fixed clamping area.

Shrink Fit Geometric parameters: Diameter, Min. / Max. length. Combination conditions: - components with a shrink fit, - names of couplings are identical, - variable clamping area.

Collet with square drive Geometric parameters: Diameter, Min. / Max. length, Min. / Max. square size. Combination conditions:- can be combined with square drive shank, - names of couplings are identical.

Collet Geometric parameters: Min. / Max. diameter, Min. / Max. length. Combination conditions: - components with a Weldon shank, - names of couplings are identical, - drill chuck can be represented, - variable clamping area.

Weldon Geometric parameters: Diameter, Length. Combination conditions: - components with a Weldon shank, - names of couplings are identical, - suitable for Whistle Notch, - fixed clamping area.

Weldon Shank Geometric parameters: Diameter, Min. / Max. length. Combination conditions: - components with a Weldon coupling or collet, - names of couplings are identical, - fixed clamping area (combination with Weldon coupling), - variable clamping area (combination with collet).

Square Drive Shank Geometric parameters: Diameter, Min. / Max. length, Square size. Combination conditions: - can be combined with collet with square drive, - names of couplings are identical.
