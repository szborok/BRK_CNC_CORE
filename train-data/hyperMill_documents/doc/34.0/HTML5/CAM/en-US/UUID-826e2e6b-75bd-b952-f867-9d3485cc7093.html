Generic turning feature/Turning groove

Generic features for turning operations can be used universally and are structured by means of manufacturing elements and their properties.

Geometric data of the Generic turning feature can be used within the job definition for the Rough Turning, Contour parallel Turning and Finish Turning cycles.

Geometric data of the Turning groove feature can be used within the job definition for the Groove Plunging and Groove Finishing cycles.

Example of use:

(A) Generic turning feature: (1) Outside profile, (2) Plane profile, (3) Inside profile, (4) Outside roughing profile, (5) Plane roughing profile, (6) Inside roughing profile

(B) Generic Hole

(A) Turning groove: (1) Turning side = Outside, Shape = Rectangle, (2) Turning side = Outside, Shape = Generic, (3) Turning side = Plane, Shape = Generic, (4) Turning side = Inside, Shape = Arc, (5) Groove roughing profile, (6) Left undercut profile

(B) Generic Hole
