Macros

Approach macro / Retract macro

Approach macros/retract macros are additional movements during infeed to the first milling path or during retraction after the last milling path.

Vertical (1): Linear movement in the direction of the tool axis. Enter length.

Circular (2): Approach and retract movements are performed in a quarter circle. Enter radius of the circle.

Tangential (3): Approach and retract movements are performed on the machining plane along the contour tangent. Enter length.

Ramp (4): Approach macro only. Soft cutting along the path.

Ramp length (1), Ramp angle (2), Ramp height (3)

Macros normal to surfaces: The approach and retract macros are executed in the direction of the surface normals.
