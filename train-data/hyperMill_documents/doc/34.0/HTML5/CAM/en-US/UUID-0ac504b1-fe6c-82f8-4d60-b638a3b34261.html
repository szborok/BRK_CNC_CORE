Corner Rest Machining

Optimized removal of rest material in vertical corners and adjacent bottom surfaces within pocket and pocket-like geometries with constant corner radii.

Note

This cycle is not designed for use on complex 3D free-form geometries. The 5X Rest Machining cycle is provided for this.

The cycle offers the functions of the 3D Corner Rest Machining cycle as well as an additional indexed corner machining function in which the optimal tool inclination is calculated for each corner (A). This means that there is no need to manually define individual frames for individual corners.

Tip

This function is also available for the Bullnose End Mill, Radiusmill tool type.

  • Optimized strategies for corners (B), rest material (C) and floor areas (D), depending on the machining situation and the model geometry.

  • Protects the upper model edges with an automatic milling surface extension (1).

  • Parallel machining with high-volume material removed in the area of vertical corners (2)

  • Z Level Machining with low-volume material removed for rest material close to the bottom (3)

  • Machining of the border areas of bottom surfaces that are adjacent to the rest material (4)

  • Seamless transitions between the machining areas and to the side and bottom surfaces through smooth overlapping of the toolpaths (5).
