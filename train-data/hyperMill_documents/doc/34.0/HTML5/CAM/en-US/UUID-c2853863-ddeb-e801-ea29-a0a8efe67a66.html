Macros

Approach macro / Retract macro

Additional movement during infeed to the first path or during retraction after the last path.

Radial (1): Define length.

Tangential (2): Define tangential length.

Circular (3): Define radius and arc angle.

Ramp (4): Define ramp length and ramp angle.

The ramp angle (1) refers to the turning axis (2), taking the infeed direction into account. (3) Infeed direction to the left, (4) Infeed direction to the right. (5) Ramp length.

Trim stock RIGHT / Trim stock LEFT

Stock trimming can be defined separately for the right and left sides.

Axial (1): Trimming from the end point of the contour parallel to the axis of the workpiece. Define Length (3).

Tangential (2): Trimming is carried out at a tangent to the contour. Define Length (3).
