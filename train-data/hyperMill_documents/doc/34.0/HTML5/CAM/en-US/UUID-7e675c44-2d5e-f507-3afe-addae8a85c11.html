Parameters

Tool position

Left/ Right: The tool moves to the right/left of the cross-section contour.

Major cutting edge height: Defines the offset of the contour along which the tool is guided in the direction of the Z axis.

Minor cutting edge height: Defines the depth of the undercut by an axial offset of the tool reference point.

(1) Cross-section contour, (2) Major cutting edge height, (3) Minor cutting edge height

Allowance

Side allowance (4): Remaining material in lateral direction.

Infeed

Total lateral infeed (5): Total infeed in lateral direction (lateral machining area).

Lateral infeed (6): Infeed in lateral direction.
