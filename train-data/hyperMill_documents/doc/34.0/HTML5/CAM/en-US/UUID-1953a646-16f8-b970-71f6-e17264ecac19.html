MB Fillet Milling

This cycle can be used to machine the fillets between the blade and hub surface.

Fillet milling is used to generate variable fillets or remove remaining rest material. This makes using larger tools to mill blade and hub surfaces more effective.

Strategy

Milling reference

Main blade / Splitter blade: Specify the blade you want to machine. The selection of the splitter will not be available, if it has not been defined on the geometry model.

Entry position

Due to the shorter distance from safety plane, it is better to use the leading side as the entry position.

On the splitter blade, the trailing side is better suited for use as the entry position as there is more space.

In the case of parts with splitter blades, we recommend you to use the same entry position (preferably the trailing edge) for both blades.

Infeed strategy

Spiral down: Uniform spiral movements around the blade surface starting from the blade and moving in the direction of the hub.

Spiral up: Uniform spiral movements around the blade surface starting from the hub and moving in the direction of the blade.
