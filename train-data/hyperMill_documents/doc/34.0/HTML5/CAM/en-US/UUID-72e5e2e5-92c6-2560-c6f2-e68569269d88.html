Overview of cycles

Roughing (1)

Spiral depth infeed with subsequent machining of the respective plane (lateral infeed from the outside in).

Tools: lollipop and ball mill, free holder geometries are supported.

Finish Turning (2)

Spiral or parallel machining of the tube.

Tools: Lollipop and Ball Mill, Woodruff Cutter, free holder geometries are supported.

Rest material (3)

Spiral or parallel machining of the tube's remaining rest material areas. The areas to be machined are additionally specified through a reference curve.

Tools: lollipop and ball mill, free holder geometries are supported.
