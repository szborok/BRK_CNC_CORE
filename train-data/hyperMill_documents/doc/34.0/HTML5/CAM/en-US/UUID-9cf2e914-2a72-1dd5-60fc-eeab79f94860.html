Boundary

Specify the horizontal limit of the machining area.

Stop surfaces

Define the area of the CAD model on which no machining takes place.

You should use stop surfaces whenever the tool is not allowed to touch specific surfaces.

Offset: the offset of the stop surfaces extends the area excluded from machining by the specified value.
