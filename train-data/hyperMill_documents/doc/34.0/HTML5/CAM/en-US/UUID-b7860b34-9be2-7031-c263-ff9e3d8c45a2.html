6.5. 3X simultaneous Roughing

  • Roughing with a simultaneous swivel axis.

  • Tilt strategy can be defined using tilt point (1) or synchronization lines (2)

  • Machine complex contours with a single tool.

  • High surface quality.

  • Reduced tool wear.

  • Machining of piston heads, turbine blades, machine discs and so on.
