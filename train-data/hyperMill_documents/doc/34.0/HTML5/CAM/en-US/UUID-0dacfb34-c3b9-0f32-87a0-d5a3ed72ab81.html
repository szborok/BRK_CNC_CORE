Mirror stock

Each stock mirror is based on an existing stock and the definition of a mirror. You can either mirror individual stock models in the stock browser or all the stock models of a job list.

Mirror individual stock model

To mirror an individual stock model:

  1. Select the stock model that you want to mirror in the stock browser and choose the Mirror stock function from the shortcut menu.

  2. Specify the mirror you require or define a new mirror in the Mirror stock dialog. For information on defining a mirror, please see the Mirror section.

The mirrored stock model is displayed in the stock browser as follows: < Stock name[ mirrored]>.

Mirroring all stock in a joblist

When you mirror a job list you also have the option of mirroring the related stocks.

To mirror all stock models in a job list:

  1. Double-click the job list entry and switch to the Mirror dialog page.

  2. Set the mirror function to Active and enable the Copy option for Manufacturing parameters.

  3. Answer the question Would you like stocks to be mirrored? with Yes.

Mirrored stocks are created for all stocks used in the job list. They are displayed in the stock browser as follows: Stock name[mirrored]>.

Warning

Stock in *.vis format, Resulting stock, Cast offset stock and Compound stock cannot be mirrored. Stock models resulting from mirrors (mirrored stock) are not associative. If the original stock or the mirror plane changes, the stock models are not updated.
