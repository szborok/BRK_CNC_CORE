Strategy

Roughing infeed

One cut (1): The entire roughing infeed is achieved in one infeed movement in the X direction.

Constant chip section (2): The same quantity of material (chip section) is removed with each infeed movement to the next level.

Constant X infeed (3): Infeed is performed with constant X value.

Infeed strategy

Right to left (1): Infeed along the right flank until thread depth is reached.

Left to right (2): Infeed along the left flank until thread depth is reached.

Alternate (3): Infeed alternating along the left and right flank until thread depth is reached.

Perpendicular (4): Infeed perpendicular until thread depth is reached.

Infeed angle: Angle for infeed strategy over flanks. The infeed angle must be smaller than the flank angle.
