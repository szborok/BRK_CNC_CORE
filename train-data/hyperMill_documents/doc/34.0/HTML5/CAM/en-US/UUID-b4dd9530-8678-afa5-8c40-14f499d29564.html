Copy and insert

Copy technology data for tools and inserts.

Paste the copied technology data.

Pasting the copied data in the browser is possible for tools, inserts as well as folders on the Tools and Inserts tab.

If technology data that is being inserted already exists, a prompt is shown asking you how you wish to proceed regarding the existing data. Technology records are deemed to exist when both material and usage match that of your entry.

The New, Overwrite and Keep options are available.

The New option is used to modify the new records depending on their insertion position such as to prevent conflicts. When inserting on the Technology field (1), the new records are assigned a material that is not yet being used. When inserting on the Material field (2), the new records are assigned a usage that is not yet being used.

Use the Overwrite option to overwrite existing records. Records that do not already exist are inserted.

Use the Keep option to ensure that existing records remain unchanged. Records that do not already exist are inserted.

If materials that are in use are deleted, the technology data sets used by the material are also deleted.
