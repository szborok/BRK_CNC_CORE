Technology: Extend machining outwards

In order to take the stock model into account before machining, it may be useful to start machining a little higher. Use Start offset (7) to extend the path outwards.
Clearance mode / Clearance / Rapid Z positions

Define the type of retraction movement and the ranges for rapid movements.

Planar: (A) The retraction movements are executed in the direction of the Z axis of the frame.

The Clearance plane (1) limits the range for rapid movements. Tool movements below the clearance distance take place at the machining feedrate.

Radial: (B) The retraction movements are radial to the Z axis of the frame.

The Clearance radius limits the range for rapid movements. Tool movements below the clearance distance take place at the machining feedrate.

Start height / End height: (3) Only available in Radial mode. Define the height for the beginning (start height) and the end of the machining (end height).
