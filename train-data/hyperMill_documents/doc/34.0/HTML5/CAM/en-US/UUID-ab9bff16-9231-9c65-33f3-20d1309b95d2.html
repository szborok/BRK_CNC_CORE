6.1. Available machining cycles

Roughing: Roughing of turning stock of any shape in axial or radial machining direction.

Contour parallel Turning: Roughing of turning stock of any shape parallel to contour.

Finishing: Contour parallel finishing of workpieces of any shape based on a preceding roughing operation.

3X simultaneous Roughing: Roughing of workpieces with a simultaneous swivel axis.

3X simultaneous Finishing: Finishing of workpieces with a simultaneous swivel axis.

Groove Turning: Axial roughing of workpieces with grooves or shoulders.

Groove Plunging: Radial roughing of workpieces with grooves and shoulders.

Groove Finishing: Radial finishing of turning workpieces with grooves or shoulders.

Face Groove Turning: Radial roughing of workpieces with axial oriented grooves or shoulders. The tools used normally have three cutting edges.

Face Groove Plunging: Axial roughing of workpieces with axial oriented grooves or shoulders. The tools used normally have three cutting edges.

Face Groove Finishing: Finishing of workpieces with axial oriented grooves or shoulders.

Parting: Radial machining to separate the component from the bar stock, optionally with chamfer. Parting off can be done on the front or at the back of the workpiece.

Thread Cutting: Creation of single or multiple start, cylindrical or conical, external and internal threads with constant pitch. Infeed can be performed either with constant chip section or with constant X value.
