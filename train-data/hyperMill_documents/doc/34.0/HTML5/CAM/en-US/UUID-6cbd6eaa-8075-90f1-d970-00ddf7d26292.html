Assign material to a cutter material

  1. Select the material and then define whether the material-cutter material combination is to be used as milling technology or turning technology with a metric or inch-based measurement system.

  		2.

    Click the icon and select the required cutter material from the list.

For each cutter material, you can define the Diameter limit, Cutting speed (Vc), F/edge (Fz) and Fz drill (f) parameters as well as a measurement system.

Depending on the tool diameter, the F/edge (Fz) feedrate defined for the (upper) limiting diameter of the cutter material and the cutting speed (Vc) are incorporated into the definition of the tool.

Take technology parameters from cutter material for NC tool

To apply the F/edge (fz) and cutting speed (Vc) technology parameters from the cutter material definition for an NC tool, follow the steps below:

  1. Define a material and cutter material (including a limiting diameter, fz and Vc).

  2. On the Tools tab, select the tool you require or define a new tool.

  3. In the Technology area, select the relevant material and the associated cutter material and select Standard as the purpose.

  4. Based on the sample data contained in the above table, a defined tool diameter of 15 results in the value 0.4 for the F/edge (fz) parameter and the value 265 for the Cutting speed (Vc) parameter (see the table).
