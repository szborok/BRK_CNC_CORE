11.6. Blade Milling

Complete machining of turbine blades in several, coordinated milling cycles.

The package contains the following cycles:

  • 3D Blade Roughing

  • 5X Blade Top Milling

  • 5X Blade Swarf Cutting

  • 5X Blade Point Milling

  • 5X Blade Platform Machining

  • 5X Blade Tangent Milling
