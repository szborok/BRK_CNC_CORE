Macros

Define the boundary handling of the tool during machining.

A distinction is made between approach macros, retract macros and plunge macros. Approach and retract macros are available for all 2D Milling cycles except for face milling and for Rest Machining . Plunge macros are available for pocket milling and inclined pocketing cycles. A detailed description of the macros can be found in the chapters on the respective milling cycles.
