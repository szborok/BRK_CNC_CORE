Boundary

Specify the horizontal limit of the machining area.

Stop surfaces

Define the area of the CAD model on which no machining takes place.

You should use stop surfaces whenever the tool is not allowed to touch specific surfaces.

Offset: The offset of the stop surfaces extends the area excluded from machining by the specified value.

Trim

Trim to milling area: The milling area defined on the Setup dialog page is used to trim the toolpaths. Therefore, all toolpaths where the tool is not in contact with the selected milling area are not applicable.

Trim to stock: The stock defined on the Setup dialog page is used to trim the toolpaths.

(1) No stock selected, (2) Stock selected

Use min. trimming distance: Enable to avoid unwanted retract movements. If the section length (measured on the toolpath) is smaller than (1) or equal to (2) the Min. trimming distance (A), the toolpath is not trimmed. If the length of the section is greater than the Min. trimming distance, a trimming operation is carried out (3).
