9.11. Rest Machining

Rest machining of rest material areas from a previous contour or pocket machining job. Requirements: The top and bottom have been defined in the previous job in absolute mode. If the Contour Milling cycle is used as the reference, only one contour may be included. The possible reference cycles are listed when you call up a rest material cycle.
